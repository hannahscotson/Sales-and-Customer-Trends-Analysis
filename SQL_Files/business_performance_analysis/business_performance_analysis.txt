Business Performance Analysis
Goal: Understand how the business is doing financially.

Typical questions:
How much total sales and profit did we make last quarter/year?
Which product categories or subcategories generate the most revenue? The least profit?
What is the overall profit margin?
How are sales trending over time — month over month, or year over year?
Did discounts improve or hurt profitability?

Why? Executives care about trends and profitability.
Turn this into time-based aggregations, profit calculations, and comparisons across categories.
