Customer Analysis
Goal: Understand who’s buying and which customers drive the most value.

Typical questions:

Who are our top customers by sales or profit?
Which customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) is most profitable?
What is the average order value per customer?
How many unique customers did we serve this quarter?
Are there customers who make frequent small purchases vs. few large ones?

Why? Marketing and retention teams use this for targeting, loyalty programs, and segmentation strategies.
